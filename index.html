Introduction to Electric Vehicles

Electric Vehicles (EVs) aren't a new thing. Believe it or not, EVs have been around since the early 1800s. They were dominated by their early Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) rivals due to the battery technology and length of time it took to charge them.

Fast forward to today, with modern battery technology, and the gap to charge an EV vs. fill up an ICE vehicle is drawing closer. My goal is to educate people about current EV technology, dispel myths about EVs, list the pros and cons of owning an EV, introduce you to the different types of EVs and help you choose a model that works with your driving needs and habits.
