About me

Hello, My name is Roger. I am an avid gear-head having restored and hot-rodded many vehicles. I have also worked in the automotive world for over 25 years, most of those at a Chrysler Dealership. I have seen many changes and advancements in automotive technology over time. In fact, if you could have told me 10 years ago that I would be driving an electric car, there's no way I would have believed you! But, I've come to realize that we are destroying the planet we live on. If the human race doesn't do something before too long, future generations won't have a planet to live on. I still wouldn't call myself a 'Tree-hugger', just one person trying to make a difference in the world.
I purchased a Chevy Volt (PHEV) 5 years ago and I don't think I will ever go back to a normal Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicle. Not only are Electric Vehicles (EVs) cheaper to operate, they will save you money, in the long run, with maintenance costs. My goal, in the not too distant future, is to own a Tesla Model 3.

I also have just begun to dabble in alternative energy solutions, mainly solar. I'm slowly purchasing the components necessary to install a grid-tie solar array to charge my EV and offset my monthly electric usage. I'm sure it will be an ongoing project as I would love to build my own version of a Tesla Powerwall (A battery storage system that will store any excess power produced by the solar panels during the day and supply power at night without reliance on the grid).

Thank you very much for taking the time to check out my site. I hope you find it informative and it helps you decide first off if an EV is right for you, and if so, help you choose the right one for your lifestyle.
